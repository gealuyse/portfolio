### 01:48 – 02:14 — สร้างไฟล์ JavaScript (`js/main.js`)

**ขนาด:** ~230 บรรทัด

**สิ่งที่สร้าง:**
- **Custom Cursor System** — Smooth follow with GSAP, hover/inverted states
- **Loader Animation** — Progress bar fill → fade out → unlock scroll
- **Header Auto-Hide** — ScrollTrigger-based show/hide on scroll direction
- **Hero SVG Animation (5-Phase):**
  - Phase 1–3 (Assembly): 22 SVG fragments drift from scattered positions toward near-alignment with slight offsets
  - Phase 4 (Lock): All fragments snap to final position with `back.out(1.7)` easing
  - Phase 5 (Calm Clarity): Traces fade, hero text reveals, SVG fades to 8% opacity
- **Scroll Reveal System** — Elements with `.reveal` class animate in on scroll
- **Stagger Groups** — Cards animate in sequence with 120ms stagger
- **Entrance Animations** — Header slides in after loader completes
- **Smooth Scroll** — Nav links scroll smoothly with header offset compensation
